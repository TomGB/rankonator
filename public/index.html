The Rankonator

If you've ever found yourself ranking a list of options by hand,
you'll probably have done it by making a torturous string of decisions comparing the importance of each item against the others.
The Rankonator will break down prioritisation into individual decisions, so there's less to hold in your head at once.
If you're ranking in your head, you're probably using something like an insertion or selection algorithm.
The Rankonator uses the QuickSort algorithm, which should reduce the number of choices you have to make.
